New Office Workspace (NOW)

New Office Workspace (NOW) from Erica P on Vimeo.

Collaborated with 14 other students to design using AutoCAD, implement, test, and present assistive technology devices to meet the needs of community members with disabilities.

Projects included an automated raising wheelchair, page-turning device, and fully accessible workspace.

National 1st Place award for Outstanding Assistive Technology Design
